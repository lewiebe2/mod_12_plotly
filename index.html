Microbiologists have discovered bacteria species that have the ability to synthesize proteins that taste like beef.
Improbable as it sounds, the best synthetic beef taste may be growing in your navel!
This page contains the results of a nation-wide, anonymous survey conducted by Improbable Beef and their research partners
on bacteria species found in participant's belly buttons.
